# Worked Examples

## Example 1: Energy Storage (Full Walkthrough)

### Industry Context
Battery energy storage systems (BESS) for commercial and industrial (C&I) customers.

### Observed Constraint
A senior engineer can evaluate ~3 projects per week. For a 50kWh convenience store installation, the pre-sales engineering cost ($3,000-5,000) exceeds the project margin. Therefore, only large factories (500kWh+) were pursued.

### Opportunities Unlocked by AI

| # | Task | Why Skipped | AI Enabler | New Economics | Who Benefits | Business Model |
|---|------|-------------|------------|---------------|--------------|----------------|
| 1 | Small retail BESS feasibility | Engineer cost > margin | OCR + rule-based modeling | 2 days → 5 min per site | BESS integrator / EPC | SaaS assessment tool |
| 2 | Multi-site portfolio analysis | Data aggregation too messy | AI reads varied utility bills & EMS data | 2 weeks → 2 hours | Asset owners / REITs | Managed service |
| 3 | Retrofit/health audit for old plants | Only big plants got attention | Anomaly detection on run data | 1 day → automated monthly report | O&M providers | Subscription AHM |

| 5 | Carbon credit development | Documentation cost > carbon revenue | Auto-generate PDD & monitoring reports | $50k → $3k per project | Project developers | Volume-based service |
| 6 | Insurance risk scoring | Underwriting cost too high for small sites | AI risk model using design & location | $500 → $10 per assessment | Insurers / brokers | API-per-query |
| 7 | Global policy monitoring | Too many countries/languages | Multilingual scraping & summarization | Team of 5 → 1 manager + AI | Exporters / investors | Intelligence subscription |
| 8 | Supplier proposal comparison | Heterogeneous formats & definitions | AI normalizes specs across vendors | 1 week → 1 hour | Procurement / EPC | Decision-support SaaS |

## Example 2: Legal Services

### Observed Constraint
A junior lawyer bills $300/hour. Reviewing a 20-page NDA for a $10k startup deal loses money for the firm.

### Opportunities
- **First-pass contract review** (Pattern 4): AI flags risky clauses; lawyer reviews exceptions. Cost: $50 → viable for any deal size.
- **Regulatory radar** (Pattern 2): Track jurisdiction-specific rule changes affecting client industries. Previously required a dedicated associate.
- **Knowledge archaeology** (Pattern 7): Surface relevant clauses from decades of firm precedent instead of reinventing them.

## Example 3: Agriculture

### Observed Constraint
Agronomists cannot visit every smallholder farm. Precision agriculture was reserved for large monoculture estates.

### Opportunities
- **Remote crop health diagnosis** (Pattern 5): Satellite/drone imagery + AI identifies pests, irrigation issues, and nutrient deficiencies per small plot.
- **Localized planting advice** (Pattern 9): AI tailors recommendations using soil sensors, weather, and seed genetics for farms < 10 hectares.
- **Compliance documentation** (Pattern 11): Organic certification and export traceability for small co-ops.

## Example 4: Healthcare

### Observed Constraint
Specialist consultations are scarce and expensive. Preventive screening and follow-up care are rationed.

### Opportunities
- **Triage and pre-consultation** (Pattern 4): AI gathers history and suggests preliminary diagnoses, freeing specialists for complex cases.
- **Personalized treatment planning** (Pattern 9): AI tailors chemotherapy or physiotherapy protocols using patient genomics and lifestyle data.
- **Dormant record mining** (Pattern 5): Identify at-risk patients from unstructured clinical notes who were never flagged for screening.

## Example 5: Construction / Real Estate

### Observed Constraint
Quantity surveyors and MEP engineers cannot affordably review every small renovation or tenant improvement.

### Opportunities
- **Drawing conflict detection** (Pattern 8): AI scans architectural, structural, and MEP drawings for clashes on small projects.
- **Permit auto-generation** (Pattern 11): Populate local building permit forms from BIM models for routine projects.
- **Renovation feasibility** (Pattern 1): AI estimates retrofit costs and energy savings from photos and utility data for small commercial buildings.
